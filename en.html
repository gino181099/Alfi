Corn Tortilla with Nopal and Flaxseed

Details

Alfimexsa has developed the Corn Tortillas with Nopal and Flaxseed to accompany the tacos, whether they are pastor, suadero, sausage, basket; or whether they are quesadillas, chalupas, guajote from Hidalgo, enchiladas, enmoladas; or simply to accompany the stew of anyone who loves healthy and healthy food.

Corn Tortilla with Tomato and Chipotle Chili

Details

The Corn Tortilla with Tomato and Chipotle Chile is the most prominent recipe of the Alfimexsa Tortillero Master.

The word Chipotle comes from the Nahuati "chilpoctli" and means smoked chili, they are placed in the ripe jalapeños, morita and pasilla on grills in ovens and left to smoke until the pasta is obtained.

Alfimexsa

We are a Mexican company dedicated to the production, distribution, national and export commercialization of fresh corn products.

Our Maestro Tortillero Mr. Jose Luis Peña through the experience of more than 30 years carefully reviews, inspects and certifies the product, the process, the formula, the elaboration and the final presentation of the different products. The high specialization of our “Maestro Tortillero” gives us the seal of guarantee of a healthy product for the families of the world.

Our main products are the production of Natural Corn Tortillas, Corn Tortillas with Nopal, Flaxseed and Flax Seeds, Corn Tortillas with Avocado and Corn Tortillas with Tomato and Chipotle Chili.

Our team is present in different parts of Mexico. With the desire to export to the United States, Canada, Central America, Europe, the Middle East and Asia our White and Yellow Corn Fresh tortilla presentations.

 Quality Policies

Always produce a healthy tortilla, respecting the standards and processes of quality, innocuousness and responsibility that allow our customers to meet the expectations of our products.

Maintain national and international certifications as a testimony that guarantees the quality and food safety of what we produce. Use raw materials and materials of the best quality.

MISSION

We are a company committed to the production, distribution and national and international commercialization of Corn products of excellent quality, with the commitment to the sustainable development of our society, with the aim of creating a better world by offering a healthy lifestyle.

VISION

Satisfy the needs of all people in the world interested in our artisan products based on corn. Improve the standard of living of our customers and seek excellence in the production of corn in our suppliers. Achieve a decent and healthy quality of life for the world. Be present in every home in the world. Allow everyone who accompanies us to grow and evolve together with us.

VALUES

To be a united team with a spirit of service, teamwork, honest, transparent in the exercise of our actions. Respecting and complying with the policies of quality, justice and corporate responsibility towards our collaborators, suppliers, clients and the world. To be part of society, where all stakeholders are part of our mission and company vision.
